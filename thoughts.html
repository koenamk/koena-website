My favourite quotes and thoughts:

  • "He who has a why to live for can bear almost any how."- Nietzche (read Frankl's Man's Search for Meaning to understand why this quote is here)
  • "This too shall pass."- Marcus Aurelius
  • "Everything we hear is an opinion, not a fact. Everything we see is a perspective, not the truth."- Marcus Aurelius
  • "No artist tolerates reality."- Nietzche
  • "Art should comfort the disturbed, and disturb the comfortable."- Banksy
  • "Between what is said and not meant, and what is meant and not said, most of love is lost."- Khalil Gibran
  • "Know all the theories, master all the techniques, but as you touch a human soul, be just another human soul."- Carl Jung
  • "Every word has consequences. Every silence, too."- Jean-Paul Sartre
  • Your 'you' can change.
  • It can be scary to be free. To make the choice that you're afraid of making. To live in accordance of your will.
